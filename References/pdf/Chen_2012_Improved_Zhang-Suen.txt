## 3. Proposed Improvements
Addresses issues in the original 1984 algorithm:
- Prevents skeleton fractures in specific 3x3 diagonal patterns.
- Ensures strict single-pixel width (prevents 2x2 blocks).
- Maintains better topological consistency for complex junctions (Branch/Cross).

- Roadmap: Migration to Chen (2012) is planned to improve node classification stability in high-complexity environments (Stage 3).
